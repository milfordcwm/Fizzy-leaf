Our Story

Hello! My name is Christian Milford and I am the owner of Fizzy Leaf. I was always tired of plain water and couldn't stand the sugary drinks that flood our market today. On the other hand, so many products that claim to have 0 sugar simply replace it with something artificial — often worse for you than sugar itself!

One Christmas, I received a small carbonator and started experimenting with different drinks to carbonate. After many failures — coffee, milk, even energy drinks — I remembered how my mom used to make me hibiscus tea when I was young. It had such rich, natural flavor. So, I decided to carbonate it… and it was amazing!

From that moment, I began bringing the tea to parties, and everyone encouraged me to start selling it. After much trial, passion, and hard work, here I am — sharing Fizzy Leaf Sparkling Tea with you.

Our Flavors

Roselle Hibiscus

Roselle Hibiscus is a bold, vibrant flavor with a naturally tart floral profile. It's refreshingly balanced — full of character yet smooth and light. With less than 1 gram of sugar, 0 carbs, 0 calories, and absolutely no fake sweeteners, it's a guilt-free indulgence that feels as pure as it tastes.
